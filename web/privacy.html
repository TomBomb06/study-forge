Privacy Policy

StudyForge · Last updated July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information StudyForge ("we," "us," or "the app") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using StudyForge, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We collect the following to run the service:

  • Account information. When you sign up we store your email address and a securely hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • Study content you upload. Files, pasted text, and web or video links you submit are processed to generate your study materials and are stored so you can return to them. You can delete your study sets at any time.
  • Usage information. Basic technical data such as your plan, how many AI videos you have generated in the current billing period, and general activity needed to operate the app.

How we use your information

  • To create the study materials you request (summaries, flashcards, quizzes, tests, audio, and video).
  • To provide your account, save your library, and track your usage and subscription.
  • To process payments through our payment provider and to show or remove advertisements based on your plan.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the service.

Third-party services

We rely on trusted third parties to operate StudyForge. These include our hosting provider, our AI content providers (used to generate study materials from your input), our payment processor (used to handle subscriptions securely — we never see or store your full card details), and our advertising partner described below.

Advertising and cookies

StudyForge is free to use with advertisements. Paid subscribers do not see ads.

  • We use Google AdSense to display advertising to free-tier users.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to StudyForge and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info.
  • We store a small amount of information in your browser (local storage) to keep you signed in and to remember display preferences.

Data retention

We keep your account and study content for as long as your account is active. If you delete a study set it is removed from your library. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's privacy

StudyForge is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Your choices

  • Upgrade to a paid plan to remove advertising entirely.
  • Opt out of personalized ads using the links above.
  • Delete your study sets, or request full account deletion, at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of StudyForge after changes means you accept the updated policy.
